Caprese Salad

Ingredients

  • 3–4 ripe tomatoes (heirloom or vine-ripened), sliced 1/4 inch thick
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella, sliced to match tomato thickness
  • Fresh basil leaves (about 12–16 leaves)
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1–2 tsp balsamic vinegar or balsamic glaze (optional)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Freshly ground black pepper (to taste)

Instructions

  1. Wash and dry the tomatoes and basil. Slice the tomatoes and mozzarella into even slices.
  2. On a serving platter, arrange alternating slices of tomato and mozzarella in a single overlapping row or concentric circles.
  3. Tuck whole basil leaves between the tomato and mozzarella slices so each bite has basil.
  4. Drizzle extra-virgin olive oil evenly over the salad.
  5. If using, lightly drizzle balsamic vinegar or balsamic glaze for a touch of sweetness and acidity.
  6. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.
  7. Serve immediately at room temperature for best flavor.

Notes & Tips

For the best Caprese, use the highest-quality ripe tomatoes and fresh mozzarella you can find. If tomatoes are out of season, consider cherry tomatoes halved or a drizzle of reduced balsamic glaze for extra flavor. Serve as an appetizer, side dish, or over toasted bread for a simple bruschetta-style variation.
