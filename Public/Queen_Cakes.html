Queen Cakes

Ingredients

Dough

  • 125g unsalted butter, softened
  • 125g caster sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 150g self-raising flour
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Filling

  • 50g currants or raisins

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F). Line a 12-hole muffin tin with paper cases.
  2. Cream the butter and sugar together in a bowl until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  4. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. Fold in the flour gently, then add the milk to loosen the batter.
  6. Mix in the currants or raisins until evenly distributed.
  7. Spoon the mixture evenly into the prepared cases.
  8. Bake for 15–20 minutes, or until golden and a skewer inserted comes out clean.
  9. Allow to cool in the tin for a few minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

Notes & Tips

For extra flavor, sprinkle a little demerara sugar on top before baking. Serve with tea for a classic British treat.
