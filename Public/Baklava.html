Baklava Recipe

Ingredients

Dough

  • 1 package phyllo dough (16 oz), thawed
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted

Filling

  • 2 cups walnuts, finely chopped
  • 1 cup pistachios, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Syrup

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 cinnamon stick

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix walnuts, pistachios, sugar, and ground cinnamon in a bowl.
  3. Brush a 9x13 inch baking dish with melted butter.
  4. Layer 8 sheets of phyllo dough in the dish, brushing each sheet with butter.
  5. Sprinkle a thin layer of nut mixture over the phyllo.
  6. Repeat layering: 4 sheets phyllo (buttered), then nut mixture, until all nuts are used.
  7. Finish with 8 more sheets of phyllo, brushing each with butter.
  8. Cut baklava into diamond or square shapes with a sharp knife.
  9. Bake for 45-50 minutes, until golden and crisp.
  10. While baking, combine water, sugar, honey, lemon juice, and cinnamon stick in a saucepan. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 10 minutes. Remove cinnamon stick and let syrup cool.
  11. Pour cooled syrup evenly over hot baklava as soon as it comes out of the oven.
  12. Allow baklava to cool completely before serving.

Notes & Tips

For best flavor, let baklava rest overnight to absorb the syrup. Serve with coffee or tea.
