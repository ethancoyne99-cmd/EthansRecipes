Éclair Recipe

Ingredients

For the Choux Pastry

  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 large eggs

For the Filling (Pastry Cream)

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

For the Chocolate Glaze

  • 4 oz semisweet chocolate, chopped
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a saucepan, combine butter, water, and salt. Bring to a boil.
  3. Add flour all at once, stirring vigorously until mixture forms a ball and pulls away from the sides.
  4. Remove from heat. Let cool for 5 minutes, then beat in eggs one at a time until smooth.
  5. Transfer dough to a piping bag. Pipe 4-inch strips onto the prepared baking sheet.
  6. Bake for 10 minutes, then reduce oven to 375°F (190°C) and bake for 20 more minutes until golden. Cool completely.
  7. For the filling, heat milk in a saucepan until just simmering. In a bowl, whisk sugar, cornstarch, and egg yolks.
  8. Slowly pour hot milk into egg mixture, whisking constantly. Return mixture to saucepan and cook until thickened.
  9. Remove from heat, stir in butter and vanilla. Cool, then refrigerate until ready to use.
  10. For the glaze, heat cream and butter until just simmering. Pour over chocolate and stir until smooth.
  11. Cut cooled éclairs in half lengthwise. Fill with pastry cream, then replace tops.
  12. Spread chocolate glaze over tops. Chill until set.

Notes & Tips

For best results, fill and glaze éclairs just before serving. Store leftovers in the refrigerator.
