Bruschetta

Ingredients

Bread

  • 1 baguette (about 12 inches), sliced 1/2-inch thick
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, halved (for rubbing)

Topping

  • 4 ripe tomatoes, seeded and finely diced (about 2 cups)
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, thinly sliced
  • 1-2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil (plus more to drizzle)
  • 1 tsp balsamic vinegar or a splash (optional)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Optional: grated Parmesan or small mozzarella pieces

Instructions

  1. Prepare the topping: In a bowl combine diced tomatoes, minced garlic, sliced basil, 1 tbsp olive oil, and balsamic if using. Season with salt and pepper. Let sit 10–15 minutes to meld flavors.
  2. Toast the bread: Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C) or heat a grill pan. Arrange baguette slices on a baking sheet, brush lightly with olive oil, and toast 5–8 minutes until golden and crisp. Alternatively, grill slices until char marks appear.
  3. Rub each warm toast with the cut side of the halved garlic clove for a subtle garlic flavor.
  4. Top the toasts: Spoon the tomato mixture onto each slice just before serving so the bread remains crisp. Add a little grated Parmesan or a small mozzarella piece if desired.
  5. Finish and serve: Drizzle with a touch of extra-virgin olive oil and an optional extra splash of balsamic. Serve immediately.

Notes & Tips

Use ripe, firm tomatoes and remove excess seeds to avoid soggy bread. Assemble just before serving. For a sweeter finish, reduce balsamic to a glaze and drizzle sparingly.
